TermiX is an AI-driven Web3 operating system that democratizes access to decentralized finance by enabling users to create, combine, and share automated workflows using natural language, making Web3 fair, user-friendly, and secure for all.

Introduction

In today’s rapidly evolving digital and AI landscape, the global Robotic Process Automation (RPA) market is booming. Leading companies such as Uipath, Blue Prism, and Automation Anywhere are delving deeper into the automation field through AI technology.

According to IDC FutureScape’s “Worldwide Artificial Intelligence Market 2021” forecast, the global RPA software market will reach $3.9 billion by 2023, with a compound annual growth rate of 36%. By 2024, it is estimated that 45% of repetitive tasks will be automated or enhanced by AI and RPA. Gartner’s “2020 State of Process Automation Report” predicts that by 2022, the hyperautomation software market will grow at a compound annual growth rate of 24% to reach $600 billion, driven mainly by low-code, RPA, and AI technologies. By 2026, this market is expected to reach $1.04 trillion, with the majority of IT decision-makers believing that process automation is crucial for digital transformation.

However, Web3, as the next-generation internet paradigm, has yet to achieve the expected user growth and market breakthrough despite having wallets as an entry point. Web3 faces challenges such as unfairness, user-unfriendliness, and fragmentation, which hinder its widespread adoption.

TermiX Agent Universe (TAU)
TAU is a vibrant ecosystem featuring modular, pluggable agent components and a rich set of multi-chain atomic instructions (TAIS). Users can create their own workflows through a no-code drag-and-drop interface, dragging third-party services onto a canvas editor to create, generate, publish, and replicate their own AI instructions, which can be released to the marketplace or integrated into team workflows.

Key features include:

Pluggable Agent Components: Users can freely combine and arrange agents to create custom workflows, enhancing flexibility.
Rich Multi-Chain Atomic Instruction Set (TAIS): Supports cross-chain functionality, enabling seamless interaction with multiple blockchains.
Developer Incentives and Revenue Sharing: Encourages developers to create high-quality agents by promoting innovation through a fair revenue-sharing mechanism.
