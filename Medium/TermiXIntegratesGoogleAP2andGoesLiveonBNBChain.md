We are pleased to announce that TermiX has completed the integration of the Google Agent Payments Protocol (AP2) and is now officially live on BNB Chain. This marks TermiX’s leading position in the practical convergence of intelligent agents and blockchain-based payments. Through this integration, we have directly enabled AP2 capabilities within the TermiX SDK, allowing developers to easily build secure, agent-driven, and fully automated payment experiences on BNB Chain.

Unlocking New Value for the BNB Chain Ecosystem
Entering a New Era of Agent-Driven Commerce
Leveraging AP2’s “delegation contracts” and “verifiable credentials,” TermiX empowers AI agents to execute transactions securely on behalf of users with explicit authorization. This not only enables fully automated business processes but also ensures authenticity, authorization transparency, and complete auditability of every action.

Full Support for Web3 Assets and Advanced Payment Protocols
AP2 natively supports stablecoins and a wide range of digital assets, and enables the use of protocols such as x402 as payment rails while maintaining compatibility with traditional payment channels. This means that, through TermiX, applications on BNB Chain can now implement seamless on-chain asset payments driven by intelligent agents.

Developer-Ready Integration
With deep AP2 integration built into the advanced TermiX SDK architecture, developers and service providers in the BNB Chain ecosystem can effortlessly adopt this future-forward agent-payment layer — unlocking plug-and-play smart payment automation.

Building the Next Generation of Commercial Infrastructure
By combining intelligent agent decision-making, AP2’s standardized payment protocol, compatibility with emerging payment technologies such as x402, and BNB Chain’s high-performance blockchain foundation, TermiX demonstrates strong leadership in technical integration and innovation — shaping a new paradigm for automated commercial and value exchange models across Web3.
