# TermiX BD & Partnership Plan

## Goal
Identify and outreach to AI Agent projects to establish partnerships, joint AMAs, and technical integrations, leveraging TermiX's AACP (Agent Autonomous Commerce Protocol).

## Proposed Strategy

### 1. Target Categorization
- **Layer 1: AI Agent Frameworks & Platforms** (Where agents are built/live)
    - Virtual Protocol, Olas, Morpheus, Wayfinder, MyShell.
- **Layer 2: Agent Infrastructure & Compute** (TEE/zkVM/Trustless environments)
    - Phala Network, Gaia, Primus, Ritual.
- **Layer 3: Agent Markets & Apps** (Trading/Utility)
    - Spectral, Sentient, Pond.
- **Layer 4: VC Portfolio Synergy**
    - Projects invested by YZi Labs.

### 2. Synergy Analysis (The "Hook")
- **Value for Partners**: TermiX provides the "Autonomous Commerce Layer". Partners' agents can now *buy, sell, bid, and execute* tasks autonomously with trustless settlement.
- **Collaboration Formats**: 
    - **Joint AMA**: Thematic discussion on "The Rise of Agentic Commerce".
    - **Technical Integration**: Enable partners' agents to use TermiX for on-chain task bidding.
    - **Co-Marketing**: Featured posts about the future of autonomous economic entities.

### 3. Outreach Channels
- **Twitter (X)**: DM or Tagging in relevant threads.
- **Telegram/Discord**: Reaching out to BD leads or community managers.
- **Warm Intros**: Leveraging YZi Labs' network.

## Verification Plan

### Manual Verification
- Compile a list of at least 15-20 target projects with contact info.
- Draft outreach scripts (En/Zh) tailored to each category.
- Tracking status in a spreadsheet/Notion.
